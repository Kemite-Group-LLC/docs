## What is KemyCard?

KemyCard is a unified fintech API that gives businesses instant access to **card issuing**, **KYC verification**, **crypto wallets**, **virtual bank accounts**, and a massive **gift card catalog** — all through a single REST API. Fund everything with USDC, settle in multiple currencies.

## Why KemyCard?

- **One API, all services** — Cards, KYC, wallets, bank accounts, gift cards under a single integration
- **USDC-powered** — Fund your ops balance with USDC on Solana, we handle the rest
- **Global coverage** — Gift cards in 50+ countries, virtual bank accounts in 5+ currencies, cards accepted worldwide
- **Test mode included** — Build and test your integration with `sk_test_` keys, zero cost, zero risk
- **Instant setup** — Get your API keys and make your first call in under 2 minutes

## Base URL

```
https://api.kemycard.com/api/v1
```

## Authentication

All requests require an `Authorization` header with your API key:

```bash
Authorization: Bearer sk_test_YOUR_API_KEY
```

<Info>
  You have two types of keys:
  - `sk_test_...` for test mode (simulated data, zero impact)
  - `sk_live_...` for production (real operations)
</Info>

## Who is this API for?

- **Fintechs** looking to offer cards and financial services to their users
- **Neobanks** that need a complete card-issuing and banking infrastructure
- **Crypto platforms** that want to bridge crypto to real-world spending with cards and bank accounts
- **Marketplaces & platforms** looking to distribute gift cards or prepaid cards at scale
- **SaaS & payroll companies** that need to issue virtual cards for expense management
